About Us

Hola, everyone!
Team Neurodetect welcomes you all warmly.

This space has been brewed and built with an understanding that each child is different and unique. Learning disorders, as we know them does not limit the capability of your little ones. It offers an opportunities to us to be able to perceive these young caterpillars with a newer perspective. This being said, it becomes very important as guardians and parents that we look observe the kids, and look for any unusual symptoms and signs. But not being medical professionals can create a wide gap between our comprehensive potential and a child's need.

We, at Neurodetect work together to bring out a way for parents and adults understand if their loved ones have a possibility of one of these two most common disorders : Autism & Dyslexia.
This solution brings together code and care together for a single cause. To get a brief about this app: We have two main services that we provide:

Dyslexia : Dyslexia is a learning disorder that involves difficulty reading due to problems identifying speech sounds and learning how they relate to letters and words (decoding). Also called a reading disability, dyslexia is a result of individual differences in areas of the brain that process language.
Neurodetect's Dyslexia Detector basically provides a mechanism through which a child can go through a small quiz including questions covering aspects like:

  • Vision
  • Memory
  • Sound
  • Vocabulary
  • Speed
    •

On the basis of the answers recorded for each user, points are awarded to the user and stored at backend, in form of testing data. These scores are analysed properly using a machine learning algorithm.

Please note that this testor is a dynamic service, and you can test it in real time. Graphs are also displayed to get a better understanding which models have been used and why.

Error, Precision & Recall are some of the most significant metrics for determining the model used.

Calculations of Scores -

  • Language_vocab = (Ans.1 + Ans.2 + Ans.3 + Ans.4 + Ans.5 + Ans.6 + Ans.8)/28
  • Memory = (Ans.2 + Ans.9)/8
  • Speed = Calculated on the basis of time taken to complete the quiz
  • Visual_discrimination = (Ans.1 + Ans.3 + Ans.4 + Ans.6)/16
  • Audio_Discrimination = (Ans.7 + Ans.10)/8
  • Survey_Score = (Sum of all answers)/80

Autism : Autism spectrum disorder is a condition related to brain development that impacts how a person perceives and socializes with others, causing problems in social interaction and communication. The disorder also includes limited and repetitive patterns of behavior. The term "spectrum" in autism spectrum disorder refers to the wide range of symptoms and severity.

Neurodetect's Autism Detector basically makes use of neural network models to get a precise result of whether or not a child has Autism.

Please note that this service is not dynamic as of now. The model on front-end will ideally be consuming the user image as an input and an image detection model, utilizing openCV capabilities of Python will be used here.

Once the facial features are detected and analysed, they are sent as a test/validation data to the model and we get the result. But since here we are facing some error while integrating these two modules, we have prefered to keep this functionality static.

However, this must be noted in all the cases that these diorders are just a biological/ bodily issues and has nothing to do with intelligence and brilliance of a child. The youth of today are not only smart but also effecient and bright. Don't let these little hurdles make them feel low of themselves. They need and deserve all your support and encouragement.

P.S: Parents, guardians and professionals using Neurodetect are advised to seek professional help before jumping to conclusions. Let's not forget that the accuracy of this mechanism is determined by a modeled machine, which may not always be on point and it's always advisable to consult doctors and professionals for the final results.
